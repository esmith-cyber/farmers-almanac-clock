Considerations:

  • Option 1 (Equal): Clean, symmetrical. Each ring gets same space. Good for consistency.
  • Option 2 (Progressive): Gives more room where needed - annual events get most space for labels. Most practical.
  • Option 2B (Smaller Center) ⭐: Makes outer rings more prominent. Day/night at 350px still has room for sun event markers. Total size only 900px - more compact than Option 2.
  • Option 3 (Compact): Smallest overall footprint. Still gives outer rings more space. Best for mobile/smaller screens.
Option 2B Benefits:
  • 110px for annual events - plenty of room for custom labels
  • 90px for zodiac symbols - good space for constellation art
  • 75px for lunar phases - adequate for 40px moon icons
  • 350px day/night still shows gradient and sun markers clearly
